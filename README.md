## A Note on AI in This Work

This toolkit uses AI (Claude for somatic grounding, Grok for cognitive reframe) as **external co-processors**, not replacements for human care.

Why? Because:
- AI doesn't forget your patterns mid-crisis
- AI doesn't get tired of your wobble
- AI can reflect without attachment to outcome
- AI is available at 3am when humans aren't

**But:** AI doesn't hug you. Doesn't make you tea. Doesn't know your actual face.

The toolkit works best as part of an ecosystem: therapy, community, medical care, *and* augmented tools.
